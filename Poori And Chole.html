Want To Make Poori Chole

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)

  • 2 onions, finely chopped

  • 2 tomatoes, pureed

  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

  • 2 tsp chole masala, 1 tsp cumin seeds

  • 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder

  • 2 cups wheat flour (for pooris), salt, water

  • 2 tbsp oil

Method

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt until soft.

  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, saut√© onions until golden.

  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, and spices. Cook until oil separates.

  4. Add boiled chickpeas and some water, simmer for 20 minutes.

  5. Knead wheat flour with salt and water to make dough, roll into small balls.

  6. Flatten balls into pooris and deep fry till golden brown.

  7. Serve hot pooris with chole gravy.

Fun Fact

Poori Chole is a beloved North Indian dish often served during festivals and special occasions. In Amritsar, a special tea bag is used while cooking chole to give it a rich dark color and unique flavor.
