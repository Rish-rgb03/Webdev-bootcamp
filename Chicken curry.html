Want To Make Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken (bone-in preferred)

  • 2 onions, sliced

  • 2 tomatoes, chopped

  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

  • Whole spices: bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom

  • 2 tsp chili powder, 2 tsp turmeric, 2 tsp garam masala

  • ½ cup yogurt or coconut milk (optional)

  • 2 tbsp oil, salt, coriander leaves for garnish

Method

  1. Marinate chicken with turmeric, chili powder, and salt for 30 minutes.

  2. Heat oil, add whole spices, sauté onions till golden brown.

  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook until fragrant, then add tomatoes and cook till mushy.

  4. Add marinated chicken, fry until lightly browned.

  5. Stir in yogurt or coconut milk, cover and simmer for 20-30 minutes until chicken is tender.

  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.

Fun Fact

Chicken curry is a versatile dish found across India, with regional twists. The Kerala version highlights coconut milk and curry leaves, reflecting the local ingredients of the coast.
