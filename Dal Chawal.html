Want To Make Dal Chawal

Ingredients

  • ½ cup dal (usually toor as it is cheaper)

  • ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp salt

  • 2 tsp oil or ghee

  • 2 cup water

  • ½ cup basmati rice

  • Salt, ½ tsp ghee

  • For tempering: 2 tbsp ghee, 1 tsp cumin, 5 cloves garlic, 1 inch ginger, pinch of hing, 2 dried red chilli (Optional If you want to spend a bit more money)

Method

  1. Wash dal and rice. In a pressure cooker, add dal, turmeric, salt, oil, and water.

  2. In a separate bowl, add rice, water, salt, and ghee. Place the bowl on top of dal in the cooker. Pressure cook for 4 whistles.

  3. For tempering, heat ghee in a pan, add cumin, garlic, ginger, hing, and red chillies until aromatic. Pour the tempering over the cooked dal and mix well.

  4. Serve dal over rice, optionally with a side of mashed potato vegetable called chokha.

Fun Fact

Dal Chawal is India's most iconic comfort food. In some regions, it’s said that the dish is symbolic of simplicity and hospitality, and it was even a favorite meal of ancient Indian warriors for sustenance.
