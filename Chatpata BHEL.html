Want To Make Sasta Bhel

Ingredients

  • 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)

  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped

  • 1 large tomato, finely chopped

  • 1 small boiled potato, cubed (optional for cost-saving)

  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)

  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

  • ½ tsp chaat masala

  • ½ tsp red chili powder (or skip for milder taste)

  • Salt to taste

  • ¼ cup roasted peanuts (cheapest crunchy addition)

  • ¼ cup sev (choose local brands or use crushed potato chips or budget mixture)

  • 2 tbsp tamarind chutney (store-bought or homemade, or use lemon juice for cheapest version)

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine puffed rice, onion, tomato, and boiled potato.

  2. Add chopped green chili, coriander leaves, chaat masala, red chili powder, salt, and roasted peanuts.

  3. Add tamarind chutney (or lemon juice) and mix well.

  4. Sprinkle sev or budget chips. Toss everything to combine.

  5. Serve immediately to maintain crunchiness.

Fun Fact

Bhel is a classic Indian street snack. To save costs, many vendors use cheap local chips, mixture, or even crushed potato wafers instead of branded papdis and sev. The base is always puffed rice, making it a filling but affordable treat!
