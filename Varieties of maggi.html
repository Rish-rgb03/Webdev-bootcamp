Want To Make Maggi

Ingredients (Basic Maggi)

  • 2 packets Maggi noodles

  • 2 cups water

  • 2 tastemaker sachets (from Maggi packets)

  • Optional add-ons: grated cheese, chopped vegetables (carrots, peas, capsicum), eggs, soy sauce

Method

  1. Boil water in a pan, add Maggi noodles and cook for 2 minutes.

  2. Add tastemaker masala and stir.

  3. Cook until noodles are soft and most water has evaporated.

  4. Optional Steps Below if you crave something else .

  5. For Cheese Maggi, stir in grated cheese at the end.

  6. For Vegetable Maggi, add boiled or stir-fried vegetables while cooking noodles.

  7. For Egg Maggi, scramble eggs separately or add raw eggs into pan and cook while stirring noodles.

  8. For Chinese Maggi, add soy sauce and chopped spring onions during cooking.

Fun Fact

Maggi noodles became a staple snack all over India since the 1980s and have inspired dozens of creative variants, from Maggi Pizza to Maggi Pakora in street food stalls.
